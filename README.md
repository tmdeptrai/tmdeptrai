# 👋 About Me:
Hi, I'm Tran Minh Duong (tmd for short)

I'm a ICT Double Degree student of USTH (gen 14), exchanging program in La Rochelle, France. My passion lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and scalable software engineering.

I have a strong foundational knowledge of ML, DL, NLP, and LLM fine-tuning. Right now, my main focus is diving deep into AI Agents and mastering the practicalities of putting large language models into production through MLOps, LLMOps, and robust cloud architectures.

I'm always eager to build intelligent systems that bridge the gap between AI research and real-world, user-facing applications.
